  • 25 September 2015

    Poland to strengthen cooperation with China: new initiatives in higher education

    The ministers of higher education from 17 countries and rectors of Polish and Chinese universities participated in an education summit organized by the Ministry of Science and Higher Education. The summit ended yesterday in Warsaw. To Poland and Central and Eastern Europe it was one of the most important educational event involving China.

  • 24 September 2015

    Diamond Grant 2015: award ceremony

    Almost 80 students won scholarships of a total value exceeding PLN 14 million. The time has come to officially award the winners, selected in July.

  • Easier access to Polish higher education for international students

    International students who wish to study in Poland will be entitled to study on more advantageous rules

  • 16 June 2015

    The agreement with the United Arab Emirates

    The Minister of Science and Higher Education, Professor Lena Kolarska-Bobińska, signed on 8th June an agreement on cooperation in science and higher education with the United Arab Emirates.

  • 05 June 2015

    The First Polish-French Forum of Science and Innovation

    On June 1st, the Ministry of Science and Higher Education, together with the Embassy of France and the French Institute in Poland, organised the First Polish-French Forum of Science and Innovation. The Forum was held at the University Library in Warsaw.
