Polish Scholarly Bibliography

Declaración de intenciones
  • Create public bibliography of a person or an institution,
  • get to know the achievements of Polish scholars,
  • get to know the achievements of Polish scholarly institutions,
  • submit a journal questionnaire during an evaluation period,
  • enable users to deposit full texts of scholarly publications in central repository.
  • integrate its resources with institutional repositories of Polish universities and scientific units,
  • allow users to adjust personal and institutional bibliographies according to the requirements of other institutions.
