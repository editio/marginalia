División tipológica

marginalists vs. extractors

Adversaria, Schopenhauer - Schopenhauer-Archiv: 1411 © Universitätsbibliothek Frankfurt am Main

"This title also alludes to an opposition between two categories of note-taking writers: the “marginalists” and the “extractors”. The extractors [...] cut up the text they read and store it, in what is supposed to be a concentrated and quintessential form but is de facto a mutilated state [...] The other class of writers, the marginalists [...] do not dismember the text they read, they preserve its contextual integrity, but they brand it with idiosyncratic marks, adorn it with commentaries of all kinds, embrace it with their own writing" Ferrer, "Towards a Marginalist Economy of Textual Genesis" in: Variants 2/3: Reading notes, 2004, pp. 7-8

  • libros de notas de Schopenhauer: Cogitata, Pandectae, Spicilegia, Senilia, Foliant, Cholerabuch, Adversaria
